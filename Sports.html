Sports

With an aim to ensure every child, a holistic development, we takes pride in providing them,

Skating, Horse Riding and Swimming. The school makes efforts to provide sports facilities of

international standards, exclusively for thrill and excitement of Sports Competitions, thus

enabling the students to learn teamwork, hardwork, passion, discipline obedience etc.

at the State & National level competitions year after year.
