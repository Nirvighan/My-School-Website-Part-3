Middle School

A thematic approach is designed to stimulate the children through various

inter-active sessions and activities. An extensive use of Smart Class and

other technology aids to supplement classroom teaching. Project works in

class calls for mass participation. An equal emphasis is laid on developing a

strong sense of individuality. Field trips and excursions make learning fun.

Use of the newspaper articles, quizzes, crosswords and other learning tools

enhance general awareness is a regular feature. Lots of activity based exposure

is given to open doors towards experiential learning.
